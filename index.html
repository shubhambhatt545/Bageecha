Our Journey of Growth

From a small passion project to a thriving community of plant lovers

What started as a love for gardening in our backyard has blossomed into a mission to bring the joy of plants to every home and garden. Our story is rooted in the belief that everyone deserves to experience the peace and satisfaction that comes from nurturing life.

Our Great Story

2018

Seeds of an Idea

Started with a small collection of plants in our home garden, sharing cuttings with neighbors and friends.

2019

First Greenhouse

Built our first greenhouse and began cultivating a wider variety of plants and flowers.

2020

Online Expansion

Launched our online platform to reach plant lovers beyond our local community during challenging times.

2021

Expert Team

Welcomed certified horticulturists and experienced gardeners to our growing family.

2022

Recognition & Awards

Received the Regional Best Nursery Award and eco-friendly business certification.

2023

5,000 Happy Customers

Celebrated serving over 5,000 customers and launched our garden consultation services.

2024

New Facility

Opened our state-of-the-art facility with expanded greenhouse space and educational center.

Where We Stand Today

Today, Bageecha stands as a testament to the power of nurturing growth. With over 5,000 satisfied customers, a team of passionate experts, and a facility spanning three acres, we continue to be the trusted partner for garden enthusiasts. Our mission remains the same: to help every plant parent succeed, whether they're tending to a single succulent or managing a sprawling garden.
